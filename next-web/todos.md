## Admin 

- dashboard 
  -  User Statistics (no of students ,staff ,etc..)
  - * User Statistics:
       * Total number of users, broken down by role (STUDENT, ADMIN, STAFF, HOD, SUPPORT\_STAFF).
       * Number of verified vs. unverified users.
       * A chart showing user sign-ups over time.
   * Student Demographics:
       * Charts showing the distribution of students by:
           * Branch (AIML, ECE, CSE, etc.)
           * Semester (S1-S8)
           * Section (A, B, C, etc.)
   * Attendance Overview:
       * Overall attendance percentage for the day/week.
       * A list of students with critically low attendance.
       * Attendance trends over the semester.
   * Academic Performance:
       * Average marks in recent IA tests across different branches and semesters.
       * A summary of university exam performance.
       * Leaderboards for top-performing students.

  Detailed Views and Management

   * User Management:
       * A searchable and filterable table of all users.
       * The ability to view a user's detailed profile (personal, academic, family details, etc.).
       * Tools to verify new users, change user roles, and reset passwords.
   * Notifications:
       * A form to send global notifications or targeted notifications to specific branches, semesters, or
         sections.
       * A log of all sent notifications.
   * Assignments and Submissions:
       * A view to see all created assignments.
       * Statistics on assignment submission rates.
   * Student Activities and Achievements:
       * A feed of recent student achievements, internships, and project involvements.
       * A way to approve and showcase notable student work.
   * Timetable Management:
       * A view of the daily timetable for different classes.
       * Tools to manage and update the timetable.
   * Placement and Internships:
       * Dashboard with statistics on placements and internships.
       * List of companies that have recruited from the institution.
       * CTC packages offered.

  Analytics and Reports

   * Attendance Reports:
       * Generate attendance reports for individual students, classes, or the entire institution for a specified
         period.
   * Academic Reports:
       * Generate reports on IA test and university exam performance.
       * Compare performance across different branches and semesters.
   * Student Progress Tracking:
       * A comprehensive view of a single student's journey, including their attendance, academic performance,
         achievements, and activities over time.
   * Mentor-Mentee Tracking:
       * An overview of the mentor-mentee relationships.
       * The ability to see the progress of mentees under a specific mentor.
